FAQ

Is VoiceCraft really free?

Yes, 100% free. No signup, no credit card, no hidden fees. Convert unlimited text to speech at no cost.

Is there a character limit?

No. You can convert short sentences or entire book chapters. For very long texts (50,000+ chars), we recommend splitting into chapters for best results.

What audio format is downloaded?

All audio is downloaded as MP3 format, compatible with all devices and media players.

Can I use the audio commercially?

Yes. Audio generated by VoiceCraft can be used for commercial purposes including YouTube videos, podcasts, audiobooks, and more.

Why does the first conversion take longer?

Our free server may take 30–60 seconds to wake up after being idle. Subsequent conversions are fast.

Which accent should I use for audiobooks?

🇺🇸 US English or 🇬🇧 UK English are most popular for audiobooks and bedtime stories. Try both and pick your favorite!

Can I change the speed?

Yes! Use the Speaking Speed slider — choose from 0.5× (slowest) to 1.5× (fastest) to suit your content.
